Campout Schedule & Local Events

Monthy Campout Schedule

Troop 370 schedules monthly campouts throughout the “camping year”, together with Winter Camp and Summer Camp. Find links to campout signup forms on the Troop's Google Calendar or Facebook page. On the calendar, click on the campout/event to see details about signing up; in order to view additional details and links about the campout, you can also click "more details" after the campout window opens within the calendar.

Pine Straw Sales

The Scout Troop’s principal fund raiser for summer camp and high adventure is selling and delivering Pine Straw to neighbors in the Buckhead, Brookhaven and Sandy Springs areas. Fall Pine Straw Sales Date – November 2, 2019 Spring Pine Straw Sales Date – March 14, 2020

Merit Badge Clinic

The Troop hosts two Merit Badge Clinics each year, open to Scouts within and outside of Troop 370. Sign up at meritbadge.info Summer Merit Badge Clinic – August 18, 2019 Winter Merit Badge Clinic – February 8, 2020 (Part of Scout Weekend)

Planning Meetings
