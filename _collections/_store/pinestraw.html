About

The Troop’s principal fund raiser for summer camp and high adventure is selling and delivering pine straw to neighbors in the Buckhead, Brookhaven, and Sandy Springs areas.

We sell premium full-sized bales of pine straw.

Pine straw can be delivered to your house by scouts or picked up at the church. Click here for directions.
Scouts are available to go to the houses and spread.

Proceeds will be used to send Scouts from Troop 370 to summer camps and high adventure camps. Learn what kind of camps and high adventure Troop 370 scouts attend at our Camps/High Adventure page and our Campout Schedule & Local Events page.
