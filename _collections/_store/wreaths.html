About

In addition to selling pine straw, Troop 370 is also selling holiday wreaths to support the Scouts planning to go to Summer Camp (at Woodruff in North Georgia and The Summit in West Virginia), as well as High Adventure opportunities in Uganda, Philmont Scout Ranch in New Mexico, an Okpik Winter Adventure in Minnesota and Sea Base in the Virgin Islands.

We have two beautiful wreath options – a 25” traditional wreath with a red ribbon and a larger 36” wintergreen wreath with pine cones and a green ribbon. These wreaths are competitively priced and will be delivered to your home between November 28 and December 8.
