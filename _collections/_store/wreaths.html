About

In addition to selling pine straw, Troop 370 is also selling holiday wreaths to support the Scouts planning to go to Summer Camp (at Woodruff in North Georgia and The Summit in West Virginia), as well as High Adventure opportunities, including backpacking at Philmont Scout Ranch in New Mexico, canoeing at Northern Tier in Canada, and rafting the New River in West Virginia.

We have two beautiful wreath options – a 25” traditional wreath with a red ribbon and a larger 36” wintergreen wreath with pine cones and a green ribbon – and a classic holiday spray which is approximately 29” tall. These holiday decorations are competitively priced and will be delivered to your home between November 30 and December 8.
